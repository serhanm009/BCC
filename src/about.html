About

BCC Group was established in 1997 in Azerbaijan as a contract company providing mainly construction services. Currently, it incorporates a number of rapidly developing companies operating in many countries. BCC Group is one of the leading contractors offering clients a large range of services including engineering, procurement, construction, commissioning, installation and maintenance for civil and industrial construction projects in oil and gas industry, steel fabrication, construction and installation of modular buildings. We are also one of the largest providers of services, supplies and equipment in the Caspian region specializing on needs of construction and oil and gas sectors.
