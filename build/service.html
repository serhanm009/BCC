Supply and Services

Materials, Tools, Equipment and Plant Supply.

Equipment and material supply: BCC Group provides hydraulic, lifting, electrical and electromechanical equipment; pipes, fittings, safety devices; construction materials, electric motors, generators and pumps as well as various tools, machinery and spare parts. Working directly with many manufacturers around the world, we guarantee that we supply products of highest quality at competitive prices. We also maintain a wide assortment of materials and equipment in stock and are able to deliver them on our client's demand at short notice. BCC Group also offers leasing of variety of equipment for civil engineering, agricultural and electrical operational works.

       BCC Group has developed close long-term business partnership with many world-known manufacturing companies such as BOGE Kompressoren, NETZSCH Pumpen, ABB, Raychem, Siemens, NOV, 3M, Betntec, EATON, Divex Global, Weir, FMC, General Monitors, Piraziz International Service Solutions, OEG Offshore, Superior Products, General Board Systems MMC, GokNur Baku Ltd., Cebo Holland, Elkem and Cromwell.

       Qualified personnel working in our representative offices in Russia, Turkey, U.A.E. and China maintain direct contacts with manufacturers in these countries to ensure delivery of the products in time and, if necessary, processing of materials prior to their shipment to the customer. Consistent with our committment to ensure best-quality customer service we also provide technical support, maintenance, inspection and repair works of the supplied products.
